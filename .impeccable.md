## Design Context

### Users
Chipzo serves students, hackers, robotics builders, and startup prototypers who need electronic components quickly while they are actively building, debugging, or preparing for demos. The homepage should help them understand that critical parts are available now and can reach their bench fast.

### Brand Personality
Precise, trusted, and maker-first. The interface should feel engineered and reliable while keeping the energy of a fast hardware marketplace.

### Aesthetic Direction
Flagship landing page polish for makers. Preserve the current neo-brutalist electronics aesthetic, but refine spacing, rhythm, hierarchy, and responsiveness so the page feels intentional rather than noisy.

### Design Principles
- Make speed feel credible, not chaotic.
- Use bold surfaces sparingly and align them to a clear spacing rhythm.
- Keep maker energy through sharp contrast, direct copy, and purposeful accents.
- Favor legibility and scannability over decorative density.
- Polish responsive spacing so the page feels designed on mobile and desktop.
